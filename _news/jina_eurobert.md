Jina releases their embeddings v5 [models](https://huggingface.co/jinaai/jina-embeddings-v5-text-nano) which claim the top spot on MMTEBv2, the default multilingual IR benchmark.
The *nano* model is based on our EuroBert model which the main author state is the best small multilingual encoder backbone amongst all those they experimented with.
